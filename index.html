ROBOSECT

The Revolution of Automatic Switchgear Connections Testing

ROBOSECT aims to introduce a robotic system for the quality control of electrical panels through autonomous connection testing. This is a process that today is still performed manually, due to the diversity of components and panels to be produced. The platform is capable of autonomously performs different quality inspection tests by utilizing information provided by the most modern and widely used design software, a vision system based on artificial intelligence, and reliable collision-free trajectory planning.

Technology

CAD Interface

ROBOSECT utilizes the digital twin created by interfacing with electrical design software such as EPLAN to streamline the process and increase efficiency.

AI-Powered Vision

The AI-based vision system is able to easily identify all the components and electrical cables in the panel.

Robotic Planning & Collision Avoidance

Collision-free motion planning allows the robotic platform to move and operate in the operation space without the risk of colliding with other objects or equipment.
